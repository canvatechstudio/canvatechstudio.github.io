Privacy Policy

CandyCue is designed to be played without collecting personal information directly from you.

Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect your name, email address, contacts, photos, location, or personal files through CandyCue.

Game Data

CandyCue may store gameplay progress, coins, settings, achievements, and scores locally on your device. If you use Apple Game Center, scores and achievements may be handled by Apple according to your Apple account and Game Center settings.

Purchases

If CandyCue offers in-app purchases, payment and purchase processing is handled by Apple. CandyCue does not receive your payment card details.

Diagnostics

We may use anonymized crash or diagnostic information provided by Apple or other app stability tools to improve reliability. This information is used to identify technical issues and is not used to identify you personally.

Contact Us
